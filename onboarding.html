WELCOME TO XLIIs

We find hidden collections of Grateful Dead tapes from collectors around the world and bring them to you for a limited run.

Each collection is on loan from a different collector — someone who spent years tracking down pristine soundboard recordings and curating them around a single era.

Every three months, a new set of collections arrives.

HOW IT WORKS

Pick a collection. Spin for a random tape or browse by year, venue, or state. Turn your phone sideways during a show to see the collector's original J-card.
